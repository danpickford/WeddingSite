Children

Unfortunately we can only accommodate children from our close family, and the children that Kathryn cares for.

We hope that this gives our friends that are parents the opportunity to relax, to let their hair down and really enjoy the day!

For the children that are attending, there is a Willow maze, and slide to play on (as well as lots of open space to run around in!)

If any of the children attending have any special dietary requirements, please let us know!

If weather permits, there will also be a few games outside!

Its advised to bring a change of clothes, and also possibly wellies incase needed as there's plenty of grass for the little ones to run around on, as we can never predict the British 'Summer' weather!

Dress Code

Smart Casual (We're aiming for a relaxed day and want our guests to be comfortable!)

Swallows Oast

Swallow's Oast was originally built in the 1830's as an Oast House. It's more recently been known as a Banqueting Hall, and now also luckily for us offers weddings!

Address:

Gibbs Reed Farm
Pashley Road
Ticehurst
TN5 7HE

Web: http://www.swallowsoast.co.uk

There is plenty of spaces to park at Swallows Oast. Please follow the signs for the "carpark" when you arrive at the venue. Please don't park beside the main building on the gravel. You can safely leave your car at the venue overnight. Although please ensure you collect it again by 10am the next day!
